Welcome to True North.

For us boating is a hiatus from our normal, off-the-water life.  While it does come with its own routines and occasional pressures, they are different enough to be a welcome variation to what we have become accustomed on land. 

We hope you find enjoyment in following along with some of our boating experiences on True North I and True North II. 

The best aspect of boating on our Coastal Craft and Selene True North's has been the ability to share our experiences with friends. The consistent feedback from our many guests is how much fun they've had exploring the waters of the Pacific Northwest. 

We welcome your feedback and ideas. 

George and Sheila
